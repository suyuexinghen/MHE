## 2026 Publications

### 1. Perturbative Variational Quantum Eigensolver via Reduced Density Matrices

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Authors** | Yuhan Zheng, Yibin Guo, Huili Zhang, Jie Liu, Xiongzhi Zeng, Xiaoxia Cai, Zhenyu Li, Jinlong Yang |
| **Date** | 2026 (Submitted April 2025) |
| **arXiv** | [2504.02340](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.02340) |
| **DOI** | 10.1103/physreva.110.XXXXX |
| **PDF** | `papers/vqe_pt_reduced_density_matrices_2504.02340.pdf` |

**Abstract:** Current noisy intermediate-scale quantum (NISQ) devices remain limited in their ability to perform accurate quantum chemistry simulations. We introduce the perturbative variational quantum eigensolver (VQE-PT), a hybrid quantum-classical algorithm that augments VQE with perturbation theory to account for electron correlation effects beyond a compact active space. The perturbative energy correction is computed from reduced density matrices, avoiding any increase in circuit depth or qubit overhead. Experimental realization on Quafu superconducting quantum processor for F₂ achieves high accuracy (mean absolute error of 1.2 millihartree) along the potential energy surface.

### 2. Simultaneous Determination of Multiple Low-Lying Energy Levels on a Superconducting Quantum Processor

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Authors** | Huili Zhang, Yibin Guo, Guanglei Xu, Yulong Feng, Jingning Zhang, Hai-feng Yu, S. P. Zhao |
| **Date** | January 2026 |
| **arXiv** | [2601.18514](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.18514) |
| **DOI** | 10.1103/PhysRevApplied.24.XXXXX |
| **PDF** | `papers/low_lying_energy_levels_2601.18514.pdf` |

**Abstract:** We report the experimental implementation of the ancilla-entangled variational quantum eigensolver (AEVQE) on the Quafu superconducting quantum cloud platform. Demonstrated on H₂ molecule and transverse-field Ising models (TFIMs). Results show potential energy curves of H₂ and ferromagnetic to paramagnetic phase transition in TFIMs. Average energy differences of 0.027 Hartree for excited states achieved.

| **Journal** | Research |
